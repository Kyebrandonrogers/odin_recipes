Spanish omelette

Image downloaded from unsplash.com, owner "Blackieshoot"

TLooking for an authentic Spanish tortilla recipe? My tortilla de patatas is the classic Spanish omelette recipe made with only 5 simple ingredients! In Spain, tortilla de patatas is the perfect breakfast, lunch, snack, or dinner--and now you can recreate it for yourself at home.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Waxy Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Onion

How to make it

  1. Prepare the potatoes:

    Peel, rinse, and thinly slice the potatoes. Pat the potatoes with a paper towel to dry them and season them with salt.

  2. Add the sliced potatoes to hot olive oil:

    making sure the potatoes are fully covered with oil. (Don't worry, you can reuse the oil a few times, so it won't go to waste.) Cook over a medium heat for 20 minutes, being careful not to let them brown too much.

  3. Prepare the eggs:

    beat the room-temperature eggs in a bowl until well mixed, and then season with salt

  4. Thinly slice the onions:

    Thinly slice the onions. This will give the tortilla the right texture and help the onions cook more quickly and evenly.

  5. Remove the potatoes:

    After cooking for about 20 minutes, remove the potatoes from the hot oil using a skimmer or spider. Drain them using a colander and let them cool for roughly 5 minutes.

  6. Using a separate pan:

    Caramelize the onions using a little olive oil, cook them for around 15-20 minutes over medium heat. Then add them to the egg mixture and gently stir them in.

  7. Add the potatoes:

    add the potatoes into the egg/onion mixture, stirring gently to stop them from scrambling the egg. Then let the mixture sit for 15 minutes.

  8. Pour the batter into the frying pan:

    Gently pour the tortilla batter into a frying pan over medium-low heat. You don't want the pan to be too hot. Gently cook the tortilla for about 6-8 minutes on the first side. You can run a spatula occasionally along the sides to make sure it's not sticking.

  9. Flip the tortilla:

    Flip the tortilla and let it cook for 6 to 8 minutes, you may leave it longer if you prefer a more cooked through tortilla.
