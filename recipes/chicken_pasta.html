Chicken Alfredo

Image downloaded from unsplash.com, owner "Engin Akyurt"

This easy Chicken Alfredo recipe includes golden pan-fried seasoned chicken breasts and tender noodles, coated in the most dreamy cream sauce ever. A showstopper meal and itâ€™s all done in just over 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil, butter(for seasoning the chicken)
  • Salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
  • Alfredo sauce
  • You may add any extra ingredients that you would like inside

How to make it

  1. Boil the spaghetti:

    Cook the Fettuccine al dente according to package instructions

  2. Cook the chicken:

    Season the chicken, then pan-fry in oil and butter, for 6 minutes a side, or until an internal temperature reaches 165 degrees F. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for a few minutes, then cut into 1/2-inch thick slices. Tent with foil, while you prepare the sauce.

  3. Make the alfredo sauce:

    Using the same pan you used to cook the chicken, the cream, butter, and seasonings are incorporated and simmered on medium-low heat until thickened, then the Parmesan is added in until melted and smooth.

  4. Assemble:

    Drain the pasta, reserving some of the liquid to loosen the sauce (only if necessary.) Toss immediately with the Alfredo sauce. Divide the pasta among serving bowls and top with a few slices of cooked chicken. Garnish with parsley, more Parmesan, and black pepper if desired.
